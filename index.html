About us

Get spain closer!

Originally from Barcelona, Spain, Barcelona Street Food team is passionate about food and delighted to share our cuisine with you.

After the successful opening of The Croquetas Bar, we are thrilled to share our second street food concept with people from Perth.

Barcelona Street Food is about sharing Catalan modern cuisine fusionized with flavours of around the globe.
